ABOUT

Emotional control headquarters that exist in everyone's head. Five emotions of joy, sadness, outburst, feisty, and timid working hard on a Bulchulju night there. For "Riley," who has to adapt to the new environment after moving, she signals her emotions busier than ever, but when "joy" and "sadness" leave the headquarters by accident, a big change comes in Riley's mind. In order for "Riley" to regain its former form, "joy" and "sadness" must return to the headquarters! However, in a world of great memories, the way to headquarters is tough… Will "Riley" be able to be happy again?
