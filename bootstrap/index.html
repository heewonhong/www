OUR STORY

Birth of Mac: From an amazing beginning to the present

Make-Up Art Cosmetics (M·A·C) first started in Toronto, Canada. Makeup artist and photographer Frank Toskan and salon operator Frank Angelo decided to create their own cosmetics brand, discouraged by the fact that well-photographed makeup products are rare. At first, I made cosmetics in the kitchen and sold them directly to fellow makeup artists, models, and photographers at the salon. Soon, stylists and fashion editors joined. As the reputation of magazines began to build and word of mouth began to spread, the two launched Mac at a store in Toronto Department Store in March 1984.
